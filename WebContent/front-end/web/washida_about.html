公司概覽 :

Since 2007, washida.co main focus on the sensibility of urban life, the pure projection of object in space, good quality of texture and the humanities of aesthetics. In these years, we have shared good designs and user experience with people, and now, we want to tell more stories.


" washida HOME" is our newly opened select shop in Taichung city, Taiwan, with the concept of home, coffee, life, art and comfortable open space. Here might be a place for you to keep a little bit unmindful, keep following our hearts freely and create a new vision for tomorrow.
